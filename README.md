# BIBI_CanopyCover
Analysis of canopy cover data collected by 2025 Stream Team as a part of the new Integrated Streams Project protocol.

### Here are the Goals Provided by Kate
To start exploring the canopy cover data we collected and compare those to metrics we can get related to forest cover and urban development in our stream buffers. I don’t have very recent landcover data for our buffers, but you could get started with the stats we do have.

The questions we’d be interested in are:
1. Basic stats – what are the mean and SD values for shade cover at our ISP sites (for the whole reach [all data], for LB, for RB, for center)
2. How do those mean and SD values correlate with wetted and bankfull width or some other measure of basin size (basin area maybe?)
3. How do those measures correlate with landcover in stream buffer areas? Could look at % urban, % forested, etc.
 
Ultimately we’d like to know if measuring canopy cover in the field is giving us any information we can’t get from aerial imagery. We want to make sure those field measurements are worth the time they take to collect.
